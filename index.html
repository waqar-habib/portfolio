A Little Bit About Me...

Born and raised in Karachi, the southern-most city of Pakistan, I moved to the States when I was just thirteen years old. Ever since I was a kid, I wanted to become a doctor, but unfortunately as my college years came closer, that image became much bleaker than it was before. After spending the first four years trying to make a spot for myself in high school, I went to Texas A&M University in College Station, Texas which happened to be the six most memorable years of my life.

Graduated in December 2018 with a Bachelor's in Biomedical Science and minors in Neuroscience and Psychology, I decided that the sciences were not my cup of tea. Ever since I started college, I had seen myself typing away behind a computer (in this case, coding) but by the time I figured I wanted to be a software developer it was too late to change my majors again for the sixth time. So, here I am, now pursuing a certificate in Full-stack Web Development with my rival school, The University of Texas, with high aims of becoming a sought-after software developer
